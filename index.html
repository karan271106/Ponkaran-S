Travel App UI

KHUSHI is a UI/UX concept for a modern travel experience platform designed to provide a smooth and engaging user journey from exploration to booking. The platform allows users to search destinations, discover hidden gems, plan trips, manage bookings and payments, and access personalized account details through an intuitive dashboard. The design focuses on clean layouts, soft shadows, strong typography, and a vibrant yet balanced color palette to create a fresh and user-friendly interface. Key screens include a landing page, destination explorer, user dashboard, trip ticket UI, and payment card UI, all crafted to feel like a real-world product. This project helped me strengthen my ability to design complete user flows while balancing aesthetics with usability.

Kinder Joy Landing Redesign

This project is a fun UI/UX concept for a Kinder Joy – DC Edition shopping experience, focused on blending playful visuals with a clean and modern interface. The design highlights a gender-based product switch for boys and girls variants, integrates DC superhero themes to enhance brand personality, and uses smooth navigation with subtle gradients for a polished feel. Key elements include toy highlights with character previews and a vibrant hero section designed to feel fresh, bright, and energetic. The overall goal was to capture the joy and surprise associated with Kinder Joy while presenting it through a refined, e-commerce-ready visual identity, helping me explore how playful branding can be balanced with usability and modern design principles.

Amul Landing Page

This project is a UI/UX concept for Amul’s Milkshake Series, focused on creating a bold and visually engaging product experience with a premium FMCG aesthetic. The design emphasizes product-centric hero layouts, where each flavour is given a distinct personality through consistent color psychology—from subtle blueberry tones to vibrant strawberry hues. It incorporates smooth gradients, clean and modern typography for a high-end feel, and floating ingredient elements to add depth and realism. The overall presentation is enhanced with high-quality mockups, aiming to deliver a polished and immersive visual experience while strengthening my ability to combine branding, aesthetics, and user-focused design in product-based interfaces.

Bike Website Design

This project is a UI/UX concept for a Royal Enfield Classic 350 landing page, designed to deliver a bold and premium visual experience while maintaining a clean and user-friendly interface. The design focuses on a strong hero layout with product-centered visuals, enhanced by smooth gradient backgrounds to create a modern aesthetic. It incorporates clean and balanced typography, along with compact specification cards for quick and easy readability. The project also follows a well-structured Figma component workflow to ensure consistency and scalability. Overall, this work helped me refine my visual consistency, layout structuring, and presentation approach while designing a product-focused interface.
